About Kv Bilaspur

Kendriya Vidyalaya Bilaspur was established on 1st April 1986 as a Civil Sector School in the premises of South Eastern Railway Bilaspur Division of Chattisgarh . The school functions with one section from class I to XII (Science, commerce and Humanities stream) with a strength of 1946 students and 61 staff. The Vidyalaya is situated one Kms away from Railway Station Our Vidyalaya had produced 100% pass percentage in Class X & XII in the consequent year Our Vidyalaya aims at academic excellence and personality development enriching National Integration and a sense of “Indianness” among the children.
